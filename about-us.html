About Art

I think that my hand-made product is far more superior than machine-made products where I take pride in what I create with my own hands. The simplicity of black and white art focuses on creating a sense of attachment to our own self and its ability to tell a story with contrast and clarity. Without the distraction of color, each line, each shape, texture takes center stage, allowing the true essence of the artwork to shine.

About Products

My drawing process is a careful blend of ink and pen techniques, each chosen to bring a unique texture and depth to my monochrome creations. For my ink art, I use a brush to create fluid, bold strokes, allowing me to capture the movement and emotion of the piece. When working with pen, I rely on my trusty micron pens to meticulously craft fine lines and intricate details, adding precision and clarity to every design. Regardless of the medium, the essence of my work remains black-and-white, where each stroke and line serves to tell a story through the power of contrast.
